Food for a family

Safe food and water supplies are a major concern following a natural disaster such as a tornado, flood, hurricane or blizzard. Texas families who live in disaster-prone areas may benefit from being prepared in the event of disaster.

Shelter for a family

The Humanitarian General Purpose Tent System (HGPTS) is crafted to be put together quickly and easily to aid in response to natural disasters and emergencies. These shelters are equipped with roofs, walls, and flooring that are connected to form a securely enclosed structure.
